Product

Today I learned is a learning feed of things Lucas has found new insight in on any given day.

Project

Design and produced a simple one-page feed that expresses an informal analysis of points of development.

Process

Ask the right questions

  • What information and feelings are we trying to convey with our story?

  • What colors, type, and imagery will facilitate the telling?

  • What architecture will complement the information?

Result

Designed and produced a simple one-page feed that expresses an informal expression of never ending growth, learning and discovery.
