Project: Sunshine Life Vacation Rental

Summary

Client

Sunshine Life vacation rental

Challenge

Design and Develop a brand and website that informs and facilitates rental bookings

Outcome

Design of a unique brand identity accompanied by a custom website with booking functionality

Deliverables
  • Branding
  • Design Direction
  • Web Design
  • Hosting and Deployment

Jekyll

Materializecss

JavaScript

HTML5

SASS

Sketch App

About

Sunshine Life is a vacation rental in beautiful Fountain Hills Arizona.

Situation

They wanted users visiting their site to get excited and book the rentals, obviously. But they wanted all this functionality within their own site rather than redirecting the user to Airbnb or Bookings.com

Challenge

Design

From a design standpoint, we needed to showcase the area without going off the rail and creating a duplicate of an area tourism site. Thus we focused on a playful and informative feel. Bulleted by a few key points of interest from the area. We facilitated further exploration by linking inline the official tourism site and other resources to make an enjoyable stay seamless without building the content and function ourselves.

Development

Develop a booking solution and experience that aligned with current audience expectations (i.e make it feel like Airbnb).


Though there are various booking solutions available through plugins, widget or third parties. Either they have poor user experiences or are only cost-effective for high volume businesses such as restaurants, hair salons etc.

Solution

After extensive research and a bit of tinkering with various possible options. It was concluded that the ideal solution was to create a custom React widget that hit google calendars API and used Stripe/Paypal for transactions.

Results

The successful design an informative brand, UI, and UX. Development of a modern website with a functional booking widget.
