About

Freebird Rides is a digital customer acquisition marketplace that allows businesses to pay for Uber rides for customers to there locations. Leveraging a suite of technologies to provide a direct return on investment and customer metrics to the business.

Situation

Freebird had an idea and wanted to move from concept to product. Their prototype required integration with data from Google, Yelp, Foursquare, Uber and Stripe's APIs.

Challenge

We were brought into Freebird right at ground level. As such, we were tasked with constant testing as the various API integrations were tested. Documentation of iterations of the user experience at the initial prototyping phase of the application. We also took an active roll in branding and production of brand assets.

Solution

We worked closely with the development team to discover the sweet spot between messaging, views and features that would give the user an optimal experience.

Results

The successful design and development from an early stage prototype to a scaled and deployed a fully functional application.
