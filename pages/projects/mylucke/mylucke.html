Project: MyLucke

Summary

Client

MyLucke is parking on-demand marketplace

Challenge

Design mobile and web portal user interface and user experience

Outcome

Design and development of multiple iterations of UI and UX

Deliverables
  • Low and High Fidelity Mockups
  • UI and UX Design, and Prototypes

Sketch App

About

MyLucke is a system that solves the parking problem. It allows for parkers and parking space owners to find spaces quickly, renting with confidence.

Situation

After beta development, MyLucke was ready to focus on the development of more fluid design and experiences.

Challenge

MyLucke needed a web portal and mobile UI design that eased, thoroughly informed and facilitated the various processes.

Solution

We began by breaking down the user needs, wants and expectations. Next we evaluated our market research and the data we gathered from users. We created a user experience that eased the user through a familiar experience drawing from many of the "on demand" best practices from Airbnb, Uber, and Spinlister.

Results

The successful production of mobile application, and web portal UI.

Deliverables

  • User Experience and User Interface Design

  • Prototyping
