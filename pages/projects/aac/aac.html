About

Advanced Aircraft Company develops and produces concept drones focused on optimizing airtime, weight, and durability. They drive to create high-performance vertical takeoff drones. The drones use solid-state fuel to enhance flight time and power output.

Situation

The goal of this project was to produce a product page, anchoring AAC developing a digital identity and informing and driving potential customers into clients. Showcasing the key value of the product and the potential benefit for customers.

Challenge

Take their unique brand and develop a site that showcases and drives audiences to stay informed and in touch as the product gains momentum.

Solution

To this end, we developed a theme that lets people know the particular market position of AAC by providing access to articles, , and interviews all readily available.

Results

The successful production of a Wordpress site.
